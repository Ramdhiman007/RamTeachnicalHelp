Common Computer & Laptop Problems

Problem: Windows Error (Blue Screen)

Solution:

  • Step 1: Restart your PC in Safe Mode
  • Step 2: Update drivers
  • Step 3: Run a virus scan
  • Step 4: If problem persists, reinstall Windows

Problem: Laptop Heating Issue

Solution:

  • Step 1: Clean the cooling fan
  • Step 2: Use a cooling pad
  • Step 3: Avoid placing the laptop on soft surfaces

Problem: Slow Computer Performance

Solution:

  • Step 1: Remove unnecessary startup programs
  • Step 2: Clear junk files
  • Step 3: Upgrade RAM if required

Problem: Internet Not Working

Solution:

  • Step 1: Check Wi-Fi / LAN connection
  • Step 2: Restart modem & router
  • Step 3: Run Network Troubleshooter
  • Step 4: Reset network settings if issue persists

Problem: Printer Not Working

Solution:

  • Step 1: Check printer cables & power
  • Step 2: Ensure paper & ink/toner are available
  • Step 3: Reinstall printer drivers
  • Step 4: Run printer troubleshooter
